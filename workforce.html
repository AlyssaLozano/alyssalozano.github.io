Workforce Funding

WorkSource Georgia and workforce sponsored enrollment pathway.

This page explains the funding path while staying careful about what Acumen can and cannot promise.

How the process works

  1. Contact your local WorkSource Georgia career center
  2. Complete eligibility review with your case manager
  3. Receive training authorization if approved
  4. Coordinate enrollment with Acumen Career Academy

Important note

Eligibility is determined exclusively by WorkSource Georgia or the relevant workforce agency. Acumen does not determine WIOA eligibility and does not guarantee funding.
